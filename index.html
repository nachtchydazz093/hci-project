Mission & Vision

Mission

To ignite youth involvement in public and civic affairs that ought to inculcate patriotism, nationalism, and other desirable values in the youth. Empowering them to play a vital role in their own development as well as in our community.

  • Foster meaningful youth engagement in governance
  • Develop future leaders through mentorship and programs
  • Create sustainable solutions for community challenges

Vision

To establish adequate, effective, responsive, and enabling mechanisms and support systems that will empower the youth and ensure their meaningful participation in governance.

By 2030, we envision:
  • 100% youth participation in community planning
  • Youth-led initiatives in all barangays
  • Recognition as a model for youth governance in the Philippines

Mandate

Our Mandate

Implement youth empowerment and development programs in accordance with Republic Act No. 10742, as amended by Republic Act No. 11768.

  • Coordinate youth development programs and activities
  • Protect and promote the rights of the youth
  • Facilitate youth leadership and civic engagement
  • Ensure alignment with national and local youth policies
